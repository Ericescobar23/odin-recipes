Lasagna

Description

A rich and creamy whole-wheat pasta dish filled layer by layer with
refreshingly fresh onions and garlic, lathered in a succulent sauce
and topped with imported, premium quality mozzarella

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pound of ground beef chuck
  • 1/2 medium onion, diced (about 3/4 cup)
